(Yet) another reason I use Tinderbox

Saturday, 17 February 2024
Topics: Tinderbox

Dave Rogers writes about a small update to Tinderbox made recently based on a conversation during a short video call between himself, me, Mark Bernstein (Tinderbox's author), and Michael Becker. Dave and I both mentioned small nits we had to pick with one of Tinderbox's tools. I even said that it was only a minor inconvenience and I felt like I was whining by even mentioning it. And yet, several days later, a new Tinderbox beta arrived with improvements solving both of our issues. I've been talking about Eastgate's exceptional support and responsiveness since at least 2007 and it remains one reason why Tinderbox is worth every penny.
