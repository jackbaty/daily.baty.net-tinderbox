    Blot.im has been down for going on two days now. I assume this is due to Amazon's AWS issues over the past 24 hours. Still, it's a bummer.

    #

    Mike Hall is on a roll with his Denote setup. I'm following it all closely because I use a nearly identical set of tooling, except mine is not nearly as well thought out or complete as his. His latest adjustment is around org-gtd and Things. I've been using org-gtd for a week or so and I'm also not sure it'll stick. Mike created a nice way to generate links between Denote notes and Things.

    #

    Speaking of Mike Hall, He recently posted about how he’s generating a searchable, static HTML version of his Denote notes so that he can look stuff up while on his phone. All I want from my Denote notes on iPhone is the ability to find and read them. I asked Mike to share his setup, which he kindly did. A few path tweaks and I was up and running in no time.
