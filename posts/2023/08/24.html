    Same photos everywhere

    Every photo I see is one of these:

    • High-contrast digital black and white "street" photo with someone walking into a shaft of light
    • High-contrast digital street photo of some people walking somewhere for no reason doing nothing interesting
    • Food of some kind
    • A landscape with the sky looking completely unnatural because Adobe discovered AI
    • An old car, especially common when shot on film
    • A macro, usually bugs or flowers
    • A bird

    There's nothing wrong with these, but are they all there is? I need to get out of my bubble.

    AI Cover Letter

    Just for kicks, I enabled the AI features in Spark Mail and asked it to write me a cover letter for a Scrum Master position. The results have depressed me in more ways than I could have imagined. Four paragraphs that contain nothing. I don't want to participate if this is what it requires.
