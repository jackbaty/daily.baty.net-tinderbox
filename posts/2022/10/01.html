    M.O.B.I.U.S. BBS

    Back in the early 1990s I started a BBS. It was built using FirstClass BBS and I called it the Michigan Online Business Information User Service (M.O.B.I.U.S.). I think the acronym came first and then I awkwardly shoehorned the title to fit.

    I had intended to charge a monthly fee for access, but honestly I had more fun designing labels and playing with the BBS software so it never went anywhere.
