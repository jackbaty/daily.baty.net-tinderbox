Dave Winer invested in Logseq

Tuesday, 11 January 2022

I see that Dave Winer has a small financial stake in Logseq.

Now I have to make a disclosure that I wish I had been able to make in November. I have made a small investment in the company. They have not announced this yet, and they asked me to wait until they do, but I feel I've already waited too long. I need people to know that I have a financial interest in the success of the company.

It's cool seeing interop happening, but it doesn't seem like disclosing early jibes with his earlier statement that, "Fourth, I like the team!"
